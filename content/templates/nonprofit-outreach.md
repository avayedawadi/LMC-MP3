# Nonprofit Outreach Template

Use this template when reaching out to a nonprofit partner for the first time or responding to a new request.

```markdown

## Body

Hi [Name],

I hope you're having a great week!

My name is [Your Name] and I'm the [Role] for the [Project Name] team at Bits of Good.

**[If responding to a request]**
Thanks for reaching out about [Topic]. I've reviewed your request and [next course of action, such as setting up a meeting].

**[If initiating contact]**
I'm writing to [give clear purpose for reaching out].

**[Next Steps/Call to Action]**
Could you please [call to action, setting up a meeting is best]?

Best,

[Your Name]
Bits of Good
```
